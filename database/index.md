### Working with Databases

#### Introduction

Goose provides a robust database management system that supports various types of databases, including SQL, NoSQL and key-value databases. This section will guide you through the basics of working with databases in Goose, including setting up connections, performing migrations, and managing data.

#### Supported Databases

- [SQL Databases](sql.md): PostgreSQL, MySQL, SQLite
- [NoSQL Databases](nsql.md): MongoDB, Cassandra
- [Key-Value Databases](kv.md): Redis, BoltDB
